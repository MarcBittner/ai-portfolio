Help · field-vault

What it is

A field-level de-identification gateway: least-privilege access to PHI by role, tokenized direct identifiers, k-anonymity risk, and a tamper-evident audit chain.

How to use

  1. Scrub a note — paste free text into PHI in free text and click Detect & redact.
  2. View k-anonymity — see the Re-identification risk panel for k_min and singletons.
  3. Request field access — pick a role, record, field, purpose in the Access console.
  4. Verify the audit — click Verify chain; try Tamper to break it, then re-verify.
