# Balcony garden journal

## April

Repotted six tomato seedlings — the variety is Black Krim this year,
after last summer's disappointing cherry experiment. Two bags of compost
hauled up four flights of stairs. My quads have opinions.

Shopping list for next weekend:
- bamboo stakes (the tall ones, 6 ft)
- twine, untreated
- a third terracotta pot for the basil
- neem oil, because the aphids found us again

## May

The Black Krims have flowers. I talk to them, which the internet assures
me raises the carbon dioxide levels and is not at all unhinged. The basil
bolted because I went to the coast for a week of book research and nobody
watered anything.

## June

First tomato. I ate it standing at the railing like a gremlin. Worth
every stair.
