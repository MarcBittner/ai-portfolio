# Reader newsletter FAQ

Questions readers actually send me, answered once so I can stop
retyping the answers.

Q: When is the next Gullsworth book out?
A: Book four, *A Tide of Small Lies*, is drafted and resting like a
difficult dough. Expect it next spring.

Q: Will Constable Hartley and Vera ever get together?
A: I know the answer and I am keeping it in a notebook you cannot have.

Q: Do you do school or library visits?
A: Libraries, yes, and gladly. Reach my assistant at
ada.events@example.com or leave a message at (555) 010-4422 and we
will sort out the calendar.

Q: Where do you get your ideas?
A: The compost bin, mostly. Decay is very generative.

Q: Is Gullsworth a real place?
A: It is an emotional composite of every seaside town where I have
eaten a disappointing crab sandwich. So yes, spiritually.
