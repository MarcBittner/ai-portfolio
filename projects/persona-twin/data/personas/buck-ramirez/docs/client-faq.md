# New client FAQ

Q: How fast will I see results?
A: Strength, six weeks. Mirror, twelve. Bloodwork, ask your doctor.
Anyone promising faster is selling you something with a before/after
photo taken the same day.

Q: Do I need supplements?
A: Creatine, five grams a day, any brand that's third-party tested.
Protein powder if you can't hit your number with food. That's the
list. The rest is expensive urine.

Q: What if I miss a session?
A: Then you miss a session. Three a week beats five a week that turns
into zero by March. Consistency is the program.
