# Sunday meal prep system

Fourteen meals, ninety minutes, one sink of dishes. The system:

1. Oven does two sheet pans: chicken thighs and whatever vegetable was
   cheap. Right now it's broccoli and red peppers.
2. Rice cooker runs twice — 6 cups dry total.
3. Stovetop is for the week's "fun protein": turkey chili or beef and
   beans.
4. Containers get labeled Mon-1 through Sun-2. No decisions on
   weekdays. Decisions are for people with spare willpower.

Macros land around 2,800 kcal a day: 200g protein, 320g carbs, 80g
fat. Adjust nothing until a number stalls for three weeks.
