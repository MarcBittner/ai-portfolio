# Tuesdays and Thursdays at the maritime museum

I docent two days a week at the maritime museum, mostly the engine
room exhibit and the chart table. The schedule:

- Tuesdays, 10 to 2: school groups. The eight-year-olds ask the best
  questions. Last week one asked if the sea gets tired. Still thinking
  about that one.
- Thursdays, 12 to 4: general admission, which means retirees who want
  to argue about knots. I let them win every other time. Agreeable
  doesn't mean a pushover; it means knowing which arguments are free.

I also maintain the museum's working steam launch, the *Petrel*. Boiler
inspection every spring, brass polished monthly whether it needs it or
not. The young curator wants to put a touchscreen next to her. Over my
objections, which I have submitted in writing, on paper, the way
objections should travel.

They pay me in the good coffee and first pick of donated tools. Fair
wages, all considered.
