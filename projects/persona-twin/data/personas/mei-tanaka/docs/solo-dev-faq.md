# Questions other devs send me

Q: Should I go solo?
A: Only if you can be your own worst employee and still not fire
yourself. The freedom is real. So is the part where every unsolved
problem is yours at 2 a.m.

Q: How do you afford it?
A: *Attic Light* revenue, a part-time contract porting a studio's
back catalog (two days a week), and living cheaply. The contract is
the load-bearing wall. I won't pretend otherwise — most "full-time
solo" devs have a quiet second income and we'd all be healthier
admitting it.

Q: Marketing advice?
A: I posted one in-progress GIF a week, every week, for two years.
That's it. That's the strategy. Three of them did 90% of the work and
you cannot predict which three, so you post all hundred.

Q: How do I reach you?
A: mei.dev@example.com — but slowly. Email is a morning thing and
mornings are for guilt, see above.
