Help · postureline

What it is

One security-posture & compliance engine over two exposure surfaces: a Warehouse surface (Snowflake-style masking-policy-as-code, CI gate, k-anonymity) and an Exposure surface (internet-intelligence estate). Both feed the same governance crosswalk — one finding maps to controls across SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001, NIST 800-53/171 and CMMC.

How to use

  1. Switch surface — use the Warehouse / Exposure tabs to change which estate is scanned.
  2. Run a scan — click Re-run scan to recompute posture, findings and the crosswalk.
  3. Read posture — grade + score, severity counts, per-framework control roll-up, and findings → controls.
  4. Warehouse extras — inspect the CI gate, generated Snowflake DDL / Terraform, and k-anonymity risk.
  5. Narrative & diff — the board/exec risk report routes through the LLM chain; the remediation diff shows posture lift.
