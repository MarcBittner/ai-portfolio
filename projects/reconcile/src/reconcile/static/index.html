Help · reconcile

What it is

Document line-item reconciliation: it extracts the lines from a change order or invoice, checks each against your baseline contract and market rates, flags overcharges, and tallies the recoverable dollars — money you can claw back — with a money-path-first review queue.

How to use

  1. Pick a document — choose a sample and Load sample, or paste a change order / invoice into the box.
  2. Analyze — click Analyze → to extract and reconcile the lines.
  3. Read the cards — document total, lines over, recoverable total, and lines needing review.
  4. Scan line items — each row shows benchmark, Δ%, recoverable $, confidence, and a verdict (ok / over / under / new scope / unknown).
  5. Work the queue — the human-review queue orders flagged lines by dollar impact with rationale.
